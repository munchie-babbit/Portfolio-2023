It's nice to meet you!
I'm Estelle.

I'm a product designer, full-stack developer, artist and more.

When I design, I always ensure that I am designing with a purpose. I continuously think about who I am building for, and what I am trying to solve. I always aim to immerse myself into a user's story.

I'm passionate about building trust as well as UX craft & culture. I'm your go-to person to craft a story, investigate the why or host a watercolour paint night :)

Case studies

What the heck happened to my inventory?

No matter the size, all Shopify merchants have inventory to mangage. But what happens when employees find a discrepency between Shopify's inventory count and the physical on hand quantity of a product?

I redesigned the Inventory History page for a mobile first experience for Shopify merchants.

How can we help the world shop more sustainably?

Neutral is a web extension that makes sustainable shopping a reality for users around the world by helping people understand their carbon footprint of shopping online.

I co-founded Neutral, a green tech startup were I lead the product direction informed by user research and environmental experts.

How can we help students budget for university?

For a lot of students, thinking about post-secondary tuition is a bridge they'll cross when they get to it. But sometimes this can be too little too late.

I conceptualized and designed TD's student budget calculator while interning at TD.
